# Miners (LPs)

**Role:** Provide USDC liquidity into vaults (one vault per market) and earn fees/emissions.&#x20;

**Requirements**

* USDC deposits; choose specific market vaults/pairs.&#x20;
* Minimum collateral targets ($200k–$1M over time); epoch‑based commitments.&#x20;

**Earning**

* **50% of trading fees** (protocol‑wide mechanic).&#x20;
* **ALPHA emissions** by deposit score = time × amount.
