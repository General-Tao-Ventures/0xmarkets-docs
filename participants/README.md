# Participants

The 0xMarkets ecosystem consists of multiple participant types, each playing a crucial role in the decentralized perpetual futures platform powered by the Cartha subnet.

---

## Overview

### Core Participants

**[Miners (LPs)](miners-lps.md)**
- Provide USDC liquidity to trading vaults
- Earn trading fees and ALPHA emissions
- Choose specific market vaults (BTCUSD, ETHUSD, etc.)
- Minimum collateral requirements apply

**[Principal Miners](principal-miners.md)**
- Registered Bittensor miners who manage liquidity positions
- Can operate privately or accept external capital
- Manage pool strategies and emission distribution
- Bridge between Bittensor and external investors

**[Federated Miners](federated-miners.md)**
- External investors who lock capital through principal miners
- Don't operate Bittensor infrastructure
- Maintain full ownership of positions via EVM wallets
- Receive profit share from principal miner's emissions

**[Traders](traders.md)**
- Open leveraged perpetual futures positions
- Pay trading fees and borrowing rates
- Access deep liquidity across multiple markets

**[Validators](validators.md)**
- Run Bittensor validators on the Cartha subnet
- Score miner performance and reliability
- Ensure network consensus and security
- Earn validation rewards

## Participation Models

### Traditional Miners (Solo)

**Who:** Individual operators with technical expertise
**Requirements:** 
- Bittensor hotkey registration
- USDC capital (100k+ minimum)
- EVM wallet

**Benefits:**
- Full control over strategy
- 100% of earned emissions
- No profit-sharing

**Best for:** Those who want independence and have sufficient capital

---

### Principal Miners (Investment Managers)

**Who:** Registered miners who accept external capital
**Requirements:**
- Everything traditional miners need
- Business structure for managing investor capital
- Communication and reporting infrastructure

**Benefits:**
- Scale beyond personal capital
- Earn management fees on external capital
- Build reputation as investment manager

**Best for:** Established miners wanting to scale operations

---

### Federated Miners (External Investors)

**Who:** Anyone with USDC and an EVM wallet
**Requirements:**
- 100k+ USDC minimum
- EVM wallet (MetaMask, etc.)
- Agreement with a principal miner

**Benefits:**
- No Bittensor infrastructure needed
- Access to subnet rewards
- Full capital control via smart contracts

**Best for:** External investors wanting exposure without technical complexity

## Choosing Your Role

### I want to provide liquidity and earn rewards...

**If you have technical expertise:**
→ Become a [Principal Miner](principal-miners.md) (operate independently)

**If you have capital but not technical expertise:**
→ Become a [Federated Miner](federated-miners.md) (lock through a principal miner)

**If you have both and want to scale:**
→ Become a [Principal Miner](principal-miners.md) in public mode (accept external capital)

### I want to trade perpetuals...

→ Become a [Trader](traders.md) (use the 0xMarkets trading interface)

### I want to validate and secure the network...

→ Become a [Validator](validators.md) (run Bittensor validation infrastructure)
